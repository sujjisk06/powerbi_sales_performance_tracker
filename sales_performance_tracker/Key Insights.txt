Key Insights 
- Sales performance varies significantly across regions, indicating uneven market penetration and growth opportunities in underperforming areas.

- A small number of products contribute the majority of total sales, highlighting product concentration and dependency risks.

- Some high-revenue products generate low profit margins, suggesting the need for pricing or cost optimization.

- Sales show clear seasonal patterns, with certain months consistently outperforming others.

- Regions with high sales volume do not always deliver high profitability, emphasizing the importance of profit-driven decision making.
